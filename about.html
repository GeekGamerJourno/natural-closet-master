About

Sue Hargreaves

My passion has always been beautiful linen fabric and sewing. After a struggle to find the perfect range for my shop, Nathans Boutique, I decided to make my own.

Now my plain colours and classic simple designs can be yours at realistic prices. Both off the rack and custom made, come in to try it on, or be measured up for your own unique piece.

Natural Closet

Natural Closet is the In House Label at Nathans Boutique.

We specialise in unique handmade clothing, using only natural fiber fabrics.

You can trust us for expert fitting and honest advice. Clothing sizes 8 to 24 or custom made.

Open Monday to Friday 9am to 4pm.
